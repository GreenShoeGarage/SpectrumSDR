Notes

Sample rate and PPM take effect on the next connect for USB hardware. The simulator applies them immediately. Direct sampling routes the ADC straight to the antenna for HF reception without an upconverter; Q branch matches the common dongle modification, and the meaningful range is 0 to 14.4 MHz. Stock dongles without the direct sampling mod will hear HF only weakly.

Recordings

IQ capture writes a 2-channel 16-bit WAV (I left, Q right), the format SDR# and friends exchange. Audio capture records the demodulated output at line level, unaffected by the volume knob. Recordings live in memory until downloaded; 10 seconds of IQ at 2.4 MS/s is roughly 96 MB.

Replay

Feed a recorded IQ WAV back through the whole receiver: spectrum, waterfall, demodulators, decoder. Loops until stopped. If the filename carries an SDR-style frequency tag (..._14265000Hz_...) the dial follows it.
